About Us!

In the early 20th century, Venkatarao Subanna one of the Great chef’s of Tamil Nadu wanted to invest in Indian restaurant business oversea. Along the same timeline his project mate Shreedhar Shastry had a tiny grocery shop in Houston Texas who also wanted to invest in Restaurant business. Venkatarao Subanna belongs to the Nadar business/landowning community. In 1992, Venkatarao Subanna on his visits to Singapore observed the functioning of the multinational fast food joints like McDonald's and used them as a model.

By the 2010, the Swadisht chain spread throughout neighborhoods in Houston. In 2012, Swadisht opened its first branch outside Houston, opening a franchise in Dallas, with a large number Indian expatriates. It eventually expanded to Paris, Frankfurt, London, New York, Toronto and Doha, Qatar, relying on the expatriate Indian population. Swadisht employs staff from India to work in their foreign branches. The overseas outlets are run by franchisees.
